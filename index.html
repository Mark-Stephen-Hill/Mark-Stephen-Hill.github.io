Mark S. Hill

Computational Biologist

I am a Computational Biologist specialising in evolutionary/population genetics and gene regulation. My typical workflow combines statistical and bioinformatic analyses of large, publicly available datasets, along with simulation and mathematical modelling approaches. I have a PhD in evolutionary genetics from University College London and have published research in leading scientific journals including PLoS Biology, Proceedings of the Royal Society: Biological Sciences, and Evolution. My recent projects have focused on integrating diverse datasets, describing properties of regulatory variants, to parameterise evolutionary simulations of gene regulation. For more detailed information please visit my research page and/or get in touch (details on contact page).
